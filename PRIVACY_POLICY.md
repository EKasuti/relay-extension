# Privacy Policy for Relay

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Introduction
Relay is a Chrome extension designed to help students at Dartmouth College manage their employment timesheets on the JobX system. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

## Data Collection
**Relay does NOT collect, store, sharing, or transmit any personal data.**

*   **No Analytics:** We do not track how you use the extension.
*   **No Server:** The extension operates entirely locally on your device (client-side). No data is sent to any external servers managed by us.
*   **No Accounts:** You do not need to create an account with us to use Relay.

## Data Usage
The extension accesses the `dartmouth.studentemployment.ngwebsolutions.com` website solely to:
1.  Read the list of available jobs from your personal dashboard.
2.  Autofill the "Start Time" and "End Time" fields on the timesheet forms based on the data you provide (via file upload or manual entry).

This interaction happens directly between your browser and the Dartmouth JobX website. Relay acts as a facilitator and does not intercept or record this data.

## Permissions
Relay requests the following permissions to function:
*   **`storage`**: Used to save your user preferences locally on your browser.
*   **`scripting`**: Used to interact with the JobX web pages to perform the autofill actions.
*   **`activeTab` / Host Permissions**: Used to detect when you are on the JobX website.

## Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the support tab on the Chrome Web Store.
